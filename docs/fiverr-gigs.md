## Cross-cutting launch tips

1. **First reviews are everything.** Run the first 5 orders at break-even or below to accumulate 5-star reviews. After 10 reviews, raise prices.

2. **Respond within 30 minutes** during the first month. Fiverr's algorithm weighs response time heavily — set up phone notifications.

3. **Update gig images every 2 weeks** for the first month. Algorithm boosts updated listings.

4. **Use the FAQ section.** Buyers who read the FAQ convert 3× higher.

5. **Set automatic Gmail filter** for `noreply@fiverr.com` → label `Fiverr/Orders` (the intake loop watches that label).
